Bookkeeping Services

Welcome to our bookkeeping services page. As a beginner in bookkeeping, we offer the following services:

  • Basic Bookkeeping: We will assist you in setting up your books, recording transactions, and organizing financial records.
  • Bank Reconciliation: We will reconcile your bank statements with your accounting records to ensure accuracy and identify discrepancies.
  • Financial Reporting: We will prepare basic financial statements, including income statements and balance sheets, to help you understand your business's financial performance.
  • Budgeting and Forecasting: We will work with you to create basic budgets and forecasts to plan for the future and make informed business decisions.
  • Tax Preparation Assistance: While we don't provide tax preparation services, we can assist you in organizing your financial information for tax purposes and liaise with tax professionals on your behalf.

If you're a small business owner or an individual in need of bookkeeping assistance, feel free to reach out to me. We're here to help you manage your finances effectively.
